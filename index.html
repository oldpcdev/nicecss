NiceCSS is an improved fork of website-templates where I increasingly evolve my experience with design and CSS. This repository is licensed under GPL V3. You can modify, share and keep the freedom at the same time.

Where is the creativity?

Today's websites are only regressing when it comes to web design

What's missing from a website these days

  • Personality
  • Nice Colors
  • Speed
  • Users Freedom
  • Stability
  • Content in the right place
  • Less User Tracking
  • Acessibility

Wait... This is just a small project!

I do not intend to campaign or lead any "anti-webdesign of today" movement. I just suggest that today's web designers see that the "modern" layout sucks for many reasons.

I currently don't have the money to host a website, so my possibilities are limited to GitHub Pages, so i can't add PHP and WordPress because i'ts impossible.

I advise you not to copy the templates

What happens when all websites look the same? Web design dies, and that kills the personality of every website. And when a human does something, it's never going to be perfect. That's why there is OSS/FOSS, everyone can fork and customize it the way they want.

Please make your own fork.
